# Content Usage Policy

This page describes the rules for using the materials published on the site  
and in the books of the **AI Developer World-Class Series**.

## Distribution
Only official links to website pages or the home page may be shared.  
Distribution of PDF files or copied content is not permitted without prior approval.

## Quoting
Short excerpts may be quoted for learning, review or educational purposes  
as long as full credit is given to Tomer Kedem  
and a clear link to the original page is included.

## Translation
The content may not be translated into other languages without permission.  
Copyright applies to translated versions as well.
